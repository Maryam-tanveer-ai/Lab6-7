Lion

Lions are powerful wild cats known as kings.

Lions are known as the “King of the Jungle”. They are powerful predators found mainly in Africa. Lions live in groups called prides and show strong social behavior. They play a major role in maintaining the balance of the ecosystem.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals.

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are highly intelligent, emotional, and social creatures. Found in Africa and Asia, they are known for their strong memory, long trunks, and family bonding behavior.

Tiger

Tigers are strong striped big cats.

Tigers are the largest members of the cat family. They are known for their orange coats with black stripes. Tigers are solitary hunters and mainly live in Asia. They are powerful, fast, and highly skilled predators.
